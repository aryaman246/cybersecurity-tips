Security Tips

  • Use strong & unique passwords.
  • Enable two-factor authentication (2FA).
  • Don’t click on suspicious links.
  • Keep software & apps updated.

Steps to Stay Secure

  1. Install antivirus and firewall.
  2. Backup your important data.
  3. Be careful on public Wi-Fi.
  4. Check website URLs before entering details.
